Haylie Tan's Portfolio

My name is Haylie and I have a passion for creating websites. I have always been fascinated by the different patterns in the natural world and in architecture. These designs range from the colors of a friendship bracelet to the triangles on a bridge. These patterns motivate and inspire me to create my own. Through my organization skills, I strive to create websites that are easy for clients to navigate. I observe patterns and develop my own minimalistic versions. I want to form aesthetically pleasing yet simple designs that are available for everyone to use. Web design enables me to contribute a new perspective and way of viewing the world.
